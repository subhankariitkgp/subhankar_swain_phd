About Me 01 // Overview

Hello! I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, working with Prof. Animesh Mukherjee. I am also a member of the Complex Networks Research Group (CNeRG).

I completed my Bachelor's degree from Government College of Engineering, Keonjhar. My research focuses on multimodal harmful content detection, with interests in Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and trustworthy AI.
